## Business Verification

### Verification Levels

| Level | Badge | Requirements |
|-------|-------|--------------|
| Unverified | None | Basic organizer account |
| Pending | Yellow | Documents submitted |
| Verified | Green | All requirements met |
| Premium | Gold | Verified + Premium subscription |

### Verification Requirements

**Step 1: Account Setup**
- Business email (company domain preferred)
- Business phone (verifiable)
- Physical address

**Step 2: Identity Verification**
- Government-issued ID of owner/representative
- Business registration document
- Proof of address (utility bill, lease)

**Step 3: Location Verification**
- Photo of business exterior
- Video walkthrough (optional)
- Google Street View confirmation

**Step 4: Review**
- Manual review by verification team
- 3-5 business day turnaround
- Decision communicated via email

### Verification Benefits

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Green Badge | Visible verification marker |
| Business Profile | Dedicated business page |
| Official Events | "Official" label on events |
| Analytics | Enhanced business insights |
| Offers | Create special promotions |
| Priority | Higher in business search |

## Offers System

### Offer Types

| Type | Description | Example |
|------|-------------|---------|
| Discount | Percentage or amount off | 20% off first visit |
| Freebie | Free item with event | Free drink |
| BOGO | Buy one, get one | Buy 2, get 1 free |

## Edge Cases

### Verification Denial
- Clear reason provided
- Appeal process available
- 30 days to resubmit

### Business Closure
- Events cancelled automatically
- Profile archived (not deleted)
- Historical data preserved

### Ownership Change
- New owner must re-verify
- Historical data transfers
- Ratings preserved
