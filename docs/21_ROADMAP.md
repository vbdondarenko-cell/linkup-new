## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Scalability | Horizontal scaling architecture |
| Performance | Caching, CDNs, optimization |
| Security | Regular audits, bug bounty |

### Market Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| User acquisition | Referral programs |
| Competition | Differentiation focus |
| Market timing | Agile response |

### Operational Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Moderation overload | Automated + community |
| Trust issues | Transparency, verification |
| Support demand | Self-service, documentation |
